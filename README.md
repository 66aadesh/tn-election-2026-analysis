# Decoding the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election
### Codebasics Resume Project Challenge | AtliQ Media

---

## Project Overview

AtliQ Media is producing a one-hour data-only TV show on the 2026 Tamil Nadu 
Assembly Election results. This project provides the data backbone for that show — 
finding the most interesting stories in the results and presenting them in a clear, 
neutral, fact-based format.

**This is a non-partisan analysis. All data sourced exclusively from the 
Election Commission of India and ECI-verified public sources.**

---

## The Story

A party registered in 2024 contested 233 of 234 constituencies in 2026.
The data tells three things about what happened next.

**Q3 — The Vote Share Story**
DMK and AIADMK held a combined 71% of votes in 2021.
In 2026 that fell to 45%. A party that did not exist in 2021 entered at 35%.

**Q2 — The Flip Story**
163 of 234 constituencies returned a different winning party in 2026 vs 2021.
TVK captured 108 of those flips — 65 from DMK, 26 from AIADMK, 11 from INC.

**Q6 — The Margin Story**
The average margin of victory fell from 11.70% in 2021 to 7.67% in 2026.
56% of TVK's 108 wins were decided by margins under 10%.
